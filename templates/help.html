August 20, 2021 by Rahul

Your heart health is important. Cardiovascular conditions such as blocked arteries from plaque build-up or a leaky heart valve can lead to more serious problems such as heart failure or even sudden death. While some people can have a blocked artery or leaky valve and never have symptoms, others develop symptoms such as tiredness, a feeling that your heart is fluttering, or breathlessness. If you notice any of these symptoms, do not delay. Talk to your doctor about seeing a heart specialist.
